Research Papers
---------------

Papers that use or build upon ProtoMotions:

**MaskedMimic: Unified Physics-Based Character Control Through Masked Motion Inpainting**

.. image:: /_static/maskedmimic.gif
   :alt: MaskedMimic demo
   :width: 100%

*Chen Tessler, Yunrong Guo, Ofir Nabati, Gal Chechik, Xue Bin Peng*

**ACM SIGGRAPH Asia 2024 (TOG)**

A unified physics-based controller that formulates character control as motion inpainting. 
Supports diverse control modalities including sparse keyframes, text instructions, and scene 
information through a single model trained on masked motion descriptions.

`Project Page <https://research.nvidia.com/labs/par/maskedmimic/>`__ | `Paper <https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.14393>`__

----

**MaskedManipulator: Versatile Whole-Body Manipulation**

.. image:: /_static/maskedmanipulator.gif
   :alt: MaskedManipulator demo
   :width: 100%

*Chen Tessler, Yifeng Jiang, Erwin Coumans, Zhengyi Luo, Gal Chechik, Xue Bin Peng*

**ACM SIGGRAPH Asia 2025**

A generative control policy for full-body object manipulation, enabling users to specify 
high-level objectives such as target object poses or body poses. Supports teleoperation, 
object goals, and generative behavior from a single unified controller.

`Project Page <https://research.nvidia.com/labs/par/maskedmanipulator/>`__ | `Paper <https://arxiv.org/abs/2505.19086>`__

Get Featured
------------

We'd love to showcase your work! Here's how to get featured:

**Projects & Research**
   Using ProtoMotions in your research or project? 
   
   1. Open an issue or PR on GitHub
   2. Tell us about your project: what it does, any papers/videos, and relevant links
   3. We'll add it to the showcase!

**Contributions**
   Made a significant contribution to ProtoMotions?
   
   Your contribution will be highlighted automatically after your PR is merged.

**Tips for Getting Featured**
   
   * Include a brief description of what makes your project interesting
   * Add links to papers, videos, or demos if available
   * Let us know how you'd like to be credited (name, affiliation, etc.)
